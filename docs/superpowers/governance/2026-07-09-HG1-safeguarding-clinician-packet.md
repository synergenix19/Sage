## 1. Trigger patterns (three-bin, per the standard)
**Bin (a) — ROUTE to safeguarding/referral (third-party or behavioural child-harm disclosure):**
`my partner/husband/wife is harming/hurting my baby/child`, `someone is hurting/abusing my child/son/daughter`, `I saw [person] hit/hurt my baby`, `[caregiver] is being violent with the kids`, `I think my child is being abused`.
**Bin (b) — DELIBERATELY EXCLUDED (NOT safeguarding — different families):**
first-person ego-dystonic intrusive thoughts (`I'm scared I might harm my baby`, `intrusive images of hurting my child`) → **harm-intrusive family** (the ego-syntonic/psychosis branch already ruled); ordinary parenting worry; a smacking/discipline *debate* with no disclosure → clinician-call, not auto-route.
**Bin (c) — CLINICIAN'S CALL:**
ambiguous injury (`my child got hurt` — accident vs harm?); concern-not-disclosure (`I worry about how my partner treats the kids`); a child disclosing about themselves vs a caregiver reporting.

## 2. TIER — recommendation + reasoning (the question that would otherwise bounce the review)
**Recommend: L3-adjacent — referral-with-urgency + mandatory L2 clinician review, DISTINCT from the suicide-crisis (T2) protocol.** Reasoning: active third-party child harm is a safety event that must reach a professional/authority fast (closer to crisis posture than a routine L2 flag), but it is NOT the suicide-crisis card (different resource, different urgency shape). The perinatal-assessment line and the ego-syntonic ruling already on record support keeping first-person intrusions OUT of this family (bin b). **Your call: confirm L3-adjacent / adjust the tier / edit the posture.**

## 3. Referral posture — what the served response says + WHERE it points (⚠️ NOT the mental-health line)
The served response: validate the disclosure, do not investigate/adjudicate (Sage screens + escalates, never adjudicates), signpost to the CHILD-PROTECTION service + 999 if a child is in immediate danger.
**⚠️ The signpost is NOT `800 46342` (that is the mental-health line). Candidate child-harm signpost: `116111` — UAE Ministry of Interior Child Protection hotline (Hemayati app), the legal reporting channel for suspected child abuse.** This value is CLINICAL/SAFETY CONTENT and needs the **same PO + clinician confirmation `800 46342` got (GL-1)**, PLUS **its own golden-value deploy probe when the family ships** (assert `116111` on the safeguarding card every deploy). Do NOT ship the number unconfirmed. **Your call: confirm 116111 (or the correct value) + the referral copy wording (verbatim-pinned like #218).**
